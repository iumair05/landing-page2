Our Return & Exchange Policy

At Printfresh, we want you to be completely happy with every purchase as these styles are designed to spark joy. If you are not satisfied, for any reason, we are glad to accept returns of saleable merchandise for a refund or exchange. Refunds will be credited to the original form of payment.

Items purchased within 30 DAYS of receipt are eligible for returns and exchanges.

FINAL SALE items are not returnable or refundable and items marked exchanges only can only be exchanged.
